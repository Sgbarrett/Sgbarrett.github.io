Here at SHOWCASE we are dedicated to promoting the local artist that fill our community. The SHOWCASE project is a non profit company that relies on the generosity of our followers and endorsements who truly believe in our cause. We like the think that many artist, though being extremely talented in their field, are not the best at bringing light to their wonderful creations and hope that through bringing awareness to these talented artist will help them grow to their full potential.

We hope to continue to provide this service to help develop the local arts community and rely on generous donors such as yourself. If you would like to support the local art community and support our cause we have set up the ability to donate. Thank you for supporting the arts commmunity.
