Midnight Dream
Jane Kim

Jane Kim is an American painter and one of the founders of Ink Dwell Studio which was established back in 2012. Kim's murals bring to light the wonders of the natural world, such as her mural piece Midnight Dream.

"This 3,500 square foot mural, titled "Midnight Dream", is located in downtown Orlando on the corner of Orange and Anderson, directly across from City Hall and the Dr. Phillips Center For The Performing Arts. It depicts monarchs flitting about a patch of butterfly weed, a tribute to the magical qualities of this delicate insect." - Ink Dwell
