KILL THE NOISE MAKES DRUMS IN SERUM

Kill The Noise, a world famous producer who once was known by the alias EWUN during the drum and bass era back in the early 2000's. Around the time period that Skrillex exploded in the bass music, a lot of drum and bass producers dropped everything that they were during to pursue the new thriving genre of Dubstep. Kill the noise is an excellent producer and provides a ton of philosophical insight into production in this walkthrough of how to create drums from scratch.
