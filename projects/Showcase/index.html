Welcome!

Showcase strives to help it’s local art scene by delivering news and sharing information about upcoming local events based around local art and artists as well as providing a platform for local art students’ works to be shown.

By supporting your local community, you provide support for today's upcoming artists to be our future visionaries in the fields of art and design. This encourages the advocacy of knowledge and creativity as well as for our youth to find outlets for ways to show their own creativity and with the support that they need to make their ideas and dreams happen.
