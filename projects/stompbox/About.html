ABOUT

My name is Stephen and I'm a web developer and electronic music producer from South Florida. I set out to make Stompbox so i could share my favorite music and production tips i've found through out the years. There's a ton of knowledge available all over the internet but it's extremely easy to find yourself wasting tons of time trying to figure out what's worth it. I hope to help shine light on new experimental bass music to the masses.
