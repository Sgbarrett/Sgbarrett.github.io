RESO - SANDROCK EP

Reso is no newbie when it comes to making bass music. A bass music champion in the dubstep realm, he's still making waves with his new ep release on SlugWife, an experimental bass music record label that focus's on half time fuzed with boom bap hip hop beats and jazz samples.

MONUMAN - RATTY

Monuman is a relative newcomer when it comes to the bass music scene, but don't let ambiguity fool you, his recent album titled Provenance is absolutely fantastic. A blend between different bass music genres, the album features all types of dark bass music. My personal favorite off the album is this tune called Ratty. An ode to how dubstep used to sound like. enjoy.

PEDESTRIAN TACTICS - REDEF

One of my personal favorites as far as under rated producers are concerned. Hailing from Seattle, Washington, Pedestrian Tactics has been curating unique bass music for quite a few years now. His biggest song "ICE" was played out by major festival headliners such as Zomboy and Bassnectar. Feel free to check out his music, it's garunteed not to disapoint.
